Program- Eating Disorder Program by SGH


Treatment for the management of eating disorders such as Anorexia Nervosa, Bulimia Nervosa and Binge Eating disorder.

This programme solves the physical and psychosocial problems commonly associated with eating disorders through a holistic approach.

    Type of treatment- Eating Disorder Program by SGH

    • Outpatient treatment
      • Assessed by psychiatrist and other team members
    • Eating Disorder Meal Support (EDMS)
      • A structured lunch support group which focused on normalizing eating behaviours in a safe environment for patients.
    • Eating Disorder community Integration (EDCI)
      • Sessions for supervised patients having meals together by Occupational Therapist and a Specialty Nurse.
    • Inpatient treatment
      • Admission for more intensive treatment and care.
    • Support for Eating Disorder Singapore (SEDS)
      • A support group for survivors as well as supporters of those coping with an Eating Disorder.

    (Singapore General Hospital, 2020)
